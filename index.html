The Seven Natural Wonders of the World

What are the Seven Natural Wonders of the World?

These wonders are located all across the world. They are the Aurora Borealis which occures in Earth's high-latitude regions. The Grand Canyon which is located in Arizona, USA. The Great Barrier Reef off the coast of Queensland, Australia. The Harbor of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Mount Everest, on the border of Nepal and China. Paricutin volcano, located n the state of Michoacan, Mexico. Victoria Falls, on the border of Zambia and Zimbabwe.

Aren't these all so beautiful? Let's go explore and check them all out!

Aurora Borealis

The Aurora Borealis is a natural wonder that occurs in the Arctic and Antarctic regions. It is sweet array of blues, pinks, purples, and greens in the sky.

What a beautiful site that we get to see in the northern latitudes.
